Time Integration

Introduction

The TR-BDF2 (trapezoidal rule and backward-difference formula of order two) scheme36 is employed to overcome the numerical stiffness of the ion-neutral collision source term. The Kernel-based method37-39 is adopted to solve the Poisson equation. The above procedures are coupled into the second-order Runge-Kutta (RK2) time-integration method40
